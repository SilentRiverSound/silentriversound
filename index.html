Services

Sound plays different roles depending on the project. In some cases it needs to stay transparent and support the content. In others, it becomes part of the identity and defines how the work is perceived.

My approach is to understand that balance early and build the audio around it. This includes shaping dialogue so it remains clear and natural, developing music and sound that follow the structure of the piece, and refining the final mix so it translates across different systems.

The work moves between music production, audio post, and sound design, but the focus remains consistent: clarity, intention, and a result that holds together in context.

Audio Post & Dialogue

Clear, natural dialogue is the foundation of most narrative work. I focus on intelligibility and pacing, shaping the voice so it sits naturally within the scene. From recording to final mix, the goal is to make the message come through without distraction.

Music & Sound Design

Each project requires a different balance between music and sound. In some cases the audio supports quietly, in others it defines the identity. I develop original music and sound design that follow the structure of the piece, guiding energy, transitions, and attention.

Mixing & Mastering

A mix should translate across systems while preserving the character of the track. I work on balance, depth, and clarity to ensure the music holds together, whether it is played on headphones, speakers, or larger systems.

About

I work with artists, brands, and creators to develop sound for music, film, and narrative projects. Each project requires a different approach. In some cases the sound stays transparent and supports the content. In others, it becomes part of the identity.

My role is to understand that balance early and shape the audio accordingly. This can involve music production, dialogue work, or sound design, but also decisions around how and where sound is recorded. When needed, I work with a portable setup to capture material in spaces that add character instead of relying only on a controlled studio environment.

Depending on the project, I collaborate with musicians and creatives to expand the production beyond a single workflow. Background in audio production and audiovisual systems, with experience across music, post-production, and synchronized media.

Work spans narrative pieces, interviews, visual content, and concept-driven audio for artistic and brand contexts.
