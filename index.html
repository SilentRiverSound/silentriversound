About

I work with artists, brands, and creators to develop sound for music, film, and narrative projects. Each project requires a different approach. In some cases the sound stays transparent and supports the content. In others, it becomes part of the identity.

My role is to understand that balance early and shape the audio accordingly. This can involve music production, dialogue work, or sound design, but also decisions around how and where sound is recorded. When needed, I work with a portable setup to capture material in spaces that add character instead of relying only on a controlled studio environment.

Depending on the project, I collaborate with musicians and creatives to expand the production beyond a single workflow. Background in audio production and audiovisual systems, with experience across music, post-production, and synchronized media.

Work spans narrative pieces, interviews, visual content, and concept-driven audio for artistic and brand contexts.
